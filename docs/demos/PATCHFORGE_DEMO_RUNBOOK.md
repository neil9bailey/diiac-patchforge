# PatchForge Demo Runbook

## Purpose

This runbook defines buyer-grade demonstration scenarios for DIIaC™ PatchForge.

## Demo Boundary

PatchForge demo flows are governance demonstrations only. They do not scan, exploit, deploy patches, mutate production systems, or approve risk without accountable human review.

## Scenario 1: Critical Exploited CVE In Orion Gateway

Show:

- vulnerability queue prioritisation
- known-exploited and internet-facing indicators
- affected service mapping
- emergency change posture
- rollback evidence blocker
- signed decision pack export

## Scenario 2: Emergency Patch With Rollback Uncertainty

Show:

- emergency fast path
- rollback blocker
- human approval requirement
- pack generated with final approval false until approval event exists

## Scenario 3: OT Controller Patch Deferral

Show:

- OT asset/service scope
- safety impact evidence
- vendor-support evidence
- maintenance-window evidence
- compensating controls
- time-bound risk acceptance

## Scenario 4: Service Transition Block

Show:

- service transition readiness view
- known-exploited vulnerability blocker
- test evidence and rollback evidence requirements
- go-live block decision posture

## Scenario 5: MSP Monthly Customer Pack

Show:

- customer-facing vulnerability summary
- risk acceptance expiry warnings
- signed pack archive
- board and customer governance report exports

## Close

End every demo by showing the decision boundary: PatchForge governs decisions and evidence. Execution remains with accountable human and operational change processes.
